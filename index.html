About Leapy

It is common thing these days to see young children becoming addicted to smartphones as they turn to online education. Not only that, but parents give their phones to their children so that they can work without interruption from children. We all know that it is not that good. According to doctors, children who are addicted are more likely to develop physical and mental illnesses. For this reason, parents are trying to somehow save their children from this menace. But they know that the current education system requires a smartphone for online education. So they have nothing to do. We are introducing this app as a solution for this reason. This app's target audience is children aged 3 to 8, and it will assist parents in reducing the risk of their children becoming addicted to smartphones and developing diseases caused by modern technology.

Introduction to our system

Children's drawings are examined to learn more about their perspectives on significant issues that face the world today and in the future. The study of children's drawings began in the late 1800s and has been used for aesthetic, educational, and clinical purposes. By expressing ideas in drawings, the child's consciousness grows; it enriches the universe of imagination and broadens the scope of daily life. The system consists of measuring the intellectual level of children by their drawings. The system will recognize the number of lines and methods that a child uses to draw a picture and predict a score for the picture. There are many levels, and the child will be leveled up with the relevant score. Parents can get a huge idea about their children through this system. The main benefit that children get from this system is the development of drawing skills.
